# 📖 Altus AI Platinum: Master Instruction Manual

Welcome to the **Platinum Grade** interview assistant. Altus AI is designed to be your silent partner in high-stakes technical evaluations. Follow this guide to master the Obsidian Command Center.

## 🏁 Phase 1: Pre-Interview Prep

### 1.1 API Key Configuration
Open the **Obsidian Drawer** (Gear Icon on the right).
- Enter your **AssemblyAI** (STT) and **OpenRouter** (AI) keys.
- **Security Note:** Keys are encrypted using your system's hardware-bound storage.

### 1.2 Hardware Setup 🎙️
- In the Drawer, locate the **Audio Hardware** dropdown.
- Select your preferred microphone. For best results, use a physical headset mic or a high-quality desktop mic.
- **Hot-Swap:** If you switch mics mid-interview, Altus AI will detect the change instantly.

### 1.3 Choose Your "Brain" 🧠
- Select an **Intelligence Core** from the dashboard.
- **Claude 3.5 Sonnet:** Best for "Deep Intelligence" tasks (System Design, LeetCode Hard).
- **Gemini Flash:** Best for speed and general QA.
- **Note on Cost:** The registry labels each model's power and token usage (Free vs Paid).

## 🚀 Phase 2: Live Operation

### 2.1 Voice Calibration
1. Click the **Zap (Capture)** icon.
2. Say a few neutral sentences (e.g., "Hello, testing the audio").
3. Altus AI will lock onto your voice profile. It will now **ignore** your talking and only respond to the interviewer's voice.

### 2.2 Stealth & Ghost Mode 🌑
- **Ctrl+Shift+V (Default):** Instantly toggles window visibility.
- **Opacity Slider:** Adjust the drawer slider to make the app practically invisible on your screen while still being readable.

### 2.3 Vision Context 👁️
- If the interviewer presents a diagram or code snippet, click the **Eye (Vision)** icon or press your custom hotkey.
- The AI will "see" the screen and update its logic based on the visual evidence.
- **Auto-Vision:** Enable this in the settings if you want the AI to automatically track your screen in the background (Technical persona only).

---

## ⌨️ Phase 3: Custom Shortcuts

Go to the **Global Hotkeys** section in the Drawer.
1. Click the record button for **Vision Capture**.
2. Press your desired keys (e.g., `Alt+C`).
3. The setting is saved instantly across the operating system.

---

## ⚠️ Troubleshooting & Safety

- **Red Banner:** If you see the Red Banner, your API key or connection has failed. Check your tokens!
- **Emergency Quit (Ctrl+Shift+Q):** Instantly terminates the app and clears all temporary memory from the system.
- **Ghosting:** If the app is too visible, lower the opacity to `40%`. It becomes a "ghost" on your screen.
